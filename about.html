About Wellbeing Hub

Empowering Coventry residents to live healthier, happier lives through accessible community services, events, and support.

Our Mission

Wellbeing Hub is dedicated to connecting people in Coventry with a wide range of health, wellbeing, and community resources. Our mission is to make support accessible to everyone—regardless of age, background, or circumstance. We believe in the power of community, collaboration, and compassion to improve lives.

What We Offer

We provide free health screenings, wellbeing workshops, family activities, and support groups. Our events and services are designed to foster connection, learning, and personal growth. Whether you’re looking for advice, a friendly group, or new skills, you’ll find a welcoming space at the Wellbeing Hub.

Health Checks

Free NHS-supported health screenings and advice for all ages.

Workshops & Events

Join mindfulness, fitness, and wellbeing workshops every week.

Family Support

Parenting classes, playgroups, and family-friendly activities.

Community

Meet new friends, join clubs, and volunteer in your local area.
