Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to register?
No, walk in anytime.
Is wheelchair access available?
Yes, fully accessible.
Can I volunteer at Wellbeing Hub?
Yes. Send us a message with your interests and availability, and our team will guide you through the next steps.
Do you offer support in other languages?
We can arrange interpretation support for key appointments. Please let us know your preferred language in advance.
How soon can I get an appointment?
Most non-urgent appointments are available within 3 to 7 days, depending on the service.
