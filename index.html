Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to register?
No, walk in anytime.
Is wheelchair access available?
Yes, fully accessible.
Can I bring my children?
Yes, children are welcome and we have a play area.
Do you offer mental health support?
Yes, we have mental health professionals available for support and advice.
Is parking available?
Yes, free parking is available on site.
How do I volunteer?
You can fill out our volunteer form at the reception or contact us by email.
Are all services free?
Yes, all our core wellbeing services are completely free for Coventry residents.
Do I need to book events in advance?
Some events require advance booking. Check the events page or call us for details.
Can I cancel my booking?
Yes, you can cancel anytime by contacting us via phone or email.
Do you provide support for older adults?
Yes, we offer tailored services including gentle exercise classes, social groups, and health advice for older adults.
Can I join as a volunteer?
Absolutely! We welcome volunteers of all ages. Visit our reception or email us to get started.
