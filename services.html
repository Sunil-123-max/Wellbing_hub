Mental Health Support

Access free, confidential counselling, drop-in sessions, and peer support groups for emotional wellbeing…

▼
Full Details:

Our mental health support services include:
  • Free confidential counselling (1-to-1 sessions with trained therapists)
  • Drop-in crisis support and emotional first aid
  • Peer support groups for depression, anxiety, and stress
  • Mindfulness and self-care workshops
  • Digital mental health resources and apps
All services are provided by NHS-trained professionals and completely confidential. No referral needed—just walk in or call to book.

▼
Full Details:

We offer a variety of physical activities for all fitness levels:
  • Weekly yoga and Pilates classes (beginners to advanced)
  • Walking groups exploring local parks and trails
  • Strength and mobility training for older adults
  • Swimming sessions at discounted community rates
  • Tai Chi and martial arts for balance and confidence
  • Adapted sports and activities for people with disabilities
Classes run multiple times per week. All instructors are certified and trained to support mixed-ability groups.
